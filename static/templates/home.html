Welcome to Dota Party!

This is a small project to show whom you already played without filling forms and etc.

find your profile

Use the search bar and if do not find your account, type your account id in dotaparty.me/profiles/<account_id> url

download your matches

Request to download your matches through your profile page. Once you do that, your matches will be download as you play

wait

Dota party is small project in a small server, so it may take a while to download all matches that are requested

check your matches

In the matches pages select your account with the radion button and check with who you already played with
